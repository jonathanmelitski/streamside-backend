# Roscoe, NY — Water Features Report
**Bounding box:** -75.1, 41.8 → -74.8, 42.0  
**Sources:** USGS NHDPlus_HR · NYS DEC Division of Inland Lakes

---

## Overview

Roscoe is known as "Trout Town USA" — the confluence of the Beaver Kill and Willowemoc Creek sits at the center of this bbox. The area holds 5 DEC-classified trout streams, 43 named streams total, 33 named lakes/ponds, and 25 public parking access points.

### Regulations by Stream

**Beaver Kill** — *Special Regulations Apply*  
- All year: Catch and Release only, Artificial Lures only  
- Reach: From Sullivan County Line downstream of Roscoe, 2.5 mi to the old railroad trestle

**Willowemoc Creek** — *Special Regulations Apply*  
- All year: Catch and Release only, Artificial Lures only  
- Reach: From the second Route 17 Quickway Bridge east of Roscoe, 3.5 mi to 1,200 ft upstream of Elm Hollow Brook confluence

**Little Beaver Kill**  
- Apr 1 – Oct 15: Daily limit 5, no more than 2 over 12"  
- Oct 16 – Mar 31: Catch and Release only, Artificial Lures only  
- Reach: From Willowemoc confluence upstream to 630 yards above Benton Hollow Brook

**Russell Brook**  
- Apr 1 – Oct 15: Daily limit 3, no more than 1 over 12"  
- Oct 16 – Mar 31: Catch and Release only, Artificial Lures only  
- Reach: From Beaver Kill confluence upstream to source

**North Branch Callicoon Creek**  
- Apr 1 – Oct 15: Daily limit 3, no more than 1 over 12"  
- Oct 16 – Mar 31: Catch and Release only, Artificial Lures only  
- Reach: From Gossweyler Pond upstream to the first Lower Weissman Road bridge crossing
